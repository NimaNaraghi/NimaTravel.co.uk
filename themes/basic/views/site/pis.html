What is the study about?

Nowadays, people - who tend to plan their travel beforehand - have access to a massive volume of information on the internet. Hence, they often spend a lot of time to arrange a satisfactory travel plan by searching through various online travel services. It encouraged me to investigate how the barrier of online self-booking might be removed by reversing the travel booking procedure. For this purpose, a web application was developed based on a process as follows:

  1. The travelers register their preferences
  2. The travel suppliers offer customized travel plans to the travelers that suits them considering their preferences

Who is doing the research?

This research is being conducted by Nima Naraghi and supervised by Dr. Graeme McRobbie (Senior Lecturer at School of Engineering and Computing) as part of a qualification towards a Masters in Web and Mobile Development.

Why have I been asked to take part?

It is likely that you have experience of travel planning using online services or you may tend to plan your travels via online travel services in the future.

Do I have to take part?

It is completely your choice as to whether you take part in this study, your participation is completely voluntary. You are free to withdraw from the study up until the survey has been completed and submitted online (after which you cannot withdraw). You will not be penalized in any way if you do not decide to take part.

What are the possible disadvantages of taking part?

I hope that participating in the study will be beneficial for you, as it is likely to help us to realize that whether our proposed approach opens a new window to the future of travel planning which is more efficient and less burdensome. However, this is your choice. Please be assured that the research is anonymous, confidential and voluntary.

If I decide to take part, what will I have to do?

  1. You visit the web application
  2. You register your preference
  3. Various suppliers and travel agencies review your preferences
  4. Meanwhile, you can leave and wait for the second email that lets you know that your travel offers are ready
  5. You will comeback to the website to check the offers and take a survey to let me know if you liked the experience you have had.

The survey will take about 10 - 15 minutes to complete.

How will my responses be used and stored?

All collected data will be stored on NimaTravel.co.uk server, SurveyPlanet.com, and a password-protected laptop computer to which only the research team will have access. Collected data will be confidential and anonymous (each participant will be assigned an ID number), therefore it is not possible to associate you to any of the data or any reports on the project. Your email will be kept separately from any of the collected survey data. Besides, your email will be used only in this project. However, the results may be presented at conferences and in subject-appropriate media. In all instances, no names or any identifiable features of participants will appear. In addition, the offers that you will receive during your experience are fiction and inaccurate which are arranged only for experimental purposes. Hence, they do not reserve you any right.

Who has reviewed the study?

The study has been reviewed by experts in the field with ethical approval granted by the UWS Ethics Committee.
